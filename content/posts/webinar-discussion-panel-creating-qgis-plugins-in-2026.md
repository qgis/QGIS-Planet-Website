<h2 class="text-build-content">issues,<b> expectations and concerns! </b></h2>
<p class="text-build-content">Oslandia has been developing QGIS plugins for more than 15 years, and we would like to invite developers to webinar and discussion event<b> Tuesday June 30th at 5pm (Paris time)</b></p>
<p class="text-build-content">The goal is to share QGIS plugin developer’s experiences, our goals, habits and difficulties, as well as discuss available tools. This discussion will also be a good opportunity to share feedback on our experience as a developer, identify needs around QGIS plugins development, and explore ways to make the most of our development work.</p>
<h2 class="text-build-content">Schedule :</h2>
<ul>
<li>Oslandia – a QGIS plugin developer shares his experience and vision</li>
<li>Oslandia – quick presentation of various useful tools for QGIS development</li>
<li>Discussions – share your experience! A moderator will ask questions and you can share your stories.</li>
</ul>
<p class="text-build-content">Registration is free but mandatory.

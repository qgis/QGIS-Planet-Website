<p>Do you want to win a <a href="https://qfield.org/2019/04/09/qfield-jump-start-packages/">QField jump-start package</a> worth 550€?</p>

Follow us on Twitter and show us how you use <a href="https://twitter.com/QFieldForQGIS" rel="noopener" target="_blank">@ <strong>QFieldForQGIS</strong></a> by tagging your tweets with <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/MyQField?src=hash" rel="noopener" target="_blank"># <strong>MyQField</strong></a> and <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/dataisoutside?src=hash" rel="noopener" target="_blank"># <strong>dataisoutside</strong></a>. <li>Likes count single, retweet count double</li>

<li>We will count on 24.4.19 at 20:00 CET</li>

</ul>
